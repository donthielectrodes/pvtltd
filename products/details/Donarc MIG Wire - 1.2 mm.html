Characteristics

Donarc MIG Welding wire is Copper coated, manganese silicon wire that provides excellent spatter free weld surface and very little slag, hence reducing post weld cleaning efforts. Can be used with CO2 gas shield.

Advantages

  High deposition rate and deep penetration.
  Stable arc and very low spatter loss.
  High deposition efficiency due to very low slag formation.
  Ideally suited for unclean and rusty surfaces.

Applications

Donarc MIG Welding wire is suitable for welding of mild and medium strength carbon steel. Can be used in fabrication of ships, bridges, containers, railway rolling stock, structural and general fabrication, etc.
